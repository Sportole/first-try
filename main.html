Saral Bank is the monetary, supervisory and regulatory body of all the commercial banks. financial institutions and micro-finances.The head office is located in Baluwatar, Kathmandu. We supervise the commercial banks, development banks, finance companies and microfinance institutions in Nepal and guide monetary policy. Saral Bank oversees foreign exchange rates and the country's foreign exchange reserves. We are a member of the Asian Clearing Union.
