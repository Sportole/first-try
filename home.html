Saral Bank has introduced its VISA Debit Card by joining the hands with Nepal Investment Bank Ltd. The VISA Debit Card of Nepal Bank allows its customers to access a vast network of ATMs located in various points in Kathmandu and other major cities within Nepal displaying the VISA logo on it. The Saral VISA Debit Card allows the cardholder to withdraw cash, inquire account balances and to pay for purchases through POS transactions.
